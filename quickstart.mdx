This is the whole journey: create an account, prove you own a domain, and run a scan.
No code changes, no agent to install.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [marshell.dev/register](https://marshell.dev/register) with email and
    password, or with **GitHub** / **Google**.

    You start on the **Free** plan — 3 scans per month, no credit card. Right after
    sign-up, chifu shows a one-time **API key** banner. Copy it if you plan to use the
    API; you won't see it again. (You can skip it for now — the dashboard works without it.)

    <Tip>
      Don't see the dashboard right away? Check your inbox and click the confirmation
      link first.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a domain">
    Open **Domains** in the sidebar, click **Add domain**, and enter your hostname —
    for example `myapp.com` or `myapp.vercel.app`.

    It shows up as **Pending** until you verify ownership in the next step.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify you own it">
    Expand the domain and pick whichever method is easier for you:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="File (easiest on Vercel)">
        chifu gives you a token. Create a file at `public/chifu-token.txt` containing
        only that token, then redeploy. The file will be live at
        `https://yourdomain.com/chifu-token.txt`.

        Click **Verify**. Done.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="DNS TXT">
        Add a `TXT` record to your DNS:

        - **Host:** `_chifu.yourdomain.com`
        - **Value:** the `chifu-verify=…` string chifu shows you

        Wait a minute for DNS to propagate, then click **Verify**.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    Once it flips to **Verified** (green), you're ready to scan.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run your first scan">
    Go to **Scans → Run scan now**, pick your verified domain, and click **Scan**.

    The status updates live in **Scan history**: `Queued → Running → Completed`.
    Most static sites finish in well under a minute.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Read your report">
    Click the completed scan to open its report:

    - **Risk score** — a 0–99 gauge with a color band, so you know at a glance.
    - **Score breakdown** — the five areas we check and what hurt each one.
    - **Tech stack**, **Known vulnerabilities**, and **Other findings**.

    Dive deeper: [Risk score](/risk-score) · [Findings & fixes](/findings)
  </Step>
</Steps>
