## What the quota means

Every scan — manual or [scheduled](/schedule) — counts against your monthly quota. The
**Overview** page and the **Scans remaining** card show how many you have left and when
they reset. Quotas are per organization and reset at the start of each calendar month.

| Plan | Scans / month | Roughly |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | 3 | a few spot checks |
| **Pro** | 30 | ~1 per day |
| **Max** | 120 | ~4 per day |

## Managing your plan

Your current plan shows on **Settings → Account** and on the **Overview** page. There's
nothing to configure on Free — you're ready to scan as soon as you verify a domain.
