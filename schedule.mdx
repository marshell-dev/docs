---
title: "Scheduled scans"
description: "Your scans run on autopilot — chifu picks the times, you pick the domain."
---

You don't set up cron jobs or pick times. Every account gets **automatic
scheduled scans**: chifu spreads your plan's scans across the month and runs
them for you, so there's always a fresh report waiting.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify a domain">
    As soon as you have a [verified domain](/domains), chifu starts scheduling
    scans for it — no extra step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="chifu allocates your slots">
    It places your plan's scans on **low-traffic hours**, balanced against every
    other account so no single hour gets overloaded. Paid plans get first pick
    of the calmest slots.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scans run automatically">
    Each one lands in your [Scan history](/scans) like a manual scan. If you've
    set up a [Discord webhook](#get-notified), you get pinged with the result.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you control

The **only** thing you choose is the target. On the **Schedule** page, pick a
verified domain — and every scheduled scan switches to it. One domain, watched
on a steady cadence.

<Note>
  Times are picked for you and stay fixed. You can't drag slots around, and
  running a manual scan won't shift your scheduled ones.
</Note>

## How often scans run

Your plan sets the cadence. chifu fits the scans to your monthly quota.

| Plan | Cadence | Scans / month |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | a few times a month | 3 |
| **Pro** | ~1 per day | 30 |
| **Max** | ~4 per day | 120 |

Scheduled scans count against the same monthly quota as manual ones. If you
burn through your quota by hand early in the month, scheduled runs pause until
it resets — your times don't change, they just resume.

## Get notified

So you don't have to watch the dashboard, connect a **Discord webhook** under
**Settings → Discord**. chifu posts every completed scan and any critical
finding straight to your channel. See [API keys & notifications](/api-keys) for
the gist, or just paste your webhook URL in settings.

## Inactivity

Scheduling is meant for active projects. If nobody on your account signs in for
**over a month**, chifu pauses the account and cancels its scheduled scans to
avoid scanning abandoned sites. Just sign back in — your schedule is restored
automatically.
